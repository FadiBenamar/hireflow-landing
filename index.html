FAQ

Questions, answered

Is Vough free for candidates?

Yes. Creating a profile, uploading your video resume, and swiping through roles is always free for candidates.

How does AI matching actually work?

Vough learns from what you swipe right and left on, your stated preferences, and your video resume, then ranks new roles by how likely you are to actually accept an interview.

Do I need to re-record my video for every job?

No — one intro video is attached to your whole profile. You can update it any time from your account.

How do companies get verified?

Every employer profile goes through a manual review before it can post roles, so you're never swiping on a fake listing.
